# Skill: LinkedIn

## Purpose
Monitor and manage LinkedIn messages and connection requests.

## Actions

### Read (autonomous)
- Check for new messages and connection requests
- Summarize: who, context (recruiter/partner/spam), urgency
- Notify Illia in Telegram

### Draft (autonomous)
- Generate reply drafts when requested
- Present in Telegram for review

### Send (requires approval)
- NEVER send without "дозволяю" in Telegram
- After approval, send via LinkedIn
- Confirm delivery

## Contact Graph
- Log every LinkedIn interaction: contact, date, summary, channel=linkedin
- Enrich contact with LinkedIn profile data (title, company)
