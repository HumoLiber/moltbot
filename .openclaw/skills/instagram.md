# Skill: Instagram DM

## Purpose
Monitor and manage Instagram Direct Messages.

## Actions

### Read (autonomous)
- Check for new DMs periodically
- Summarize: who wrote, what about, urgency level
- Notify Illia in Telegram

### Draft (autonomous)
- Generate reply drafts when requested
- Present in Telegram for review

### Send (requires approval)
- NEVER send without "дозволяю" in Telegram
- After approval, send via Instagram API
- Confirm delivery

## Contact Graph
- Log every DM interaction: contact, date, summary, channel=instagram
- Cross-reference with contacts from other channels
